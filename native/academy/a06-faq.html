Before the tryout

The questions parents actually ask.

What ages can try out?
Boys and girls from the youngest squads up to U-19. If you’re not sure which group fits, just register — we’ll place your kid by age and level at the tryout.
What does it cost? Is financial aid available?
Pricing is clear and up front — no surprise fees. And there’s financial aid, because no kid should miss out over money. Ask us at the tryout; the conversation stays private.
Where and when do you train?
On Treasure Coast grounds close to home, near the site where the stadium is going up. You’ll get the exact location and weekly schedule when you register for tryouts.
My kid already plays for another club — can they still try out?
Yes. Come see what we do and how we develop players — no commitment to register. Plenty of our players came from somewhere else first.
Is there really a path to the first team?
It’s the whole point. Academy → reserve → the 2027 first team is one ladder, and the reserve is already competing. We also help players onto the US college and pro roads.
What should we bring to the tryout?
Boots, shin guards, a ball if you have one, and water. That’s it. We’ll email you the details and the date once you’re on the list.
